Hello, I'm Essme

I work at the intersection of analysis and creation. As a data analyst, I clean, model and visualise data to surface insights that drive real business decisions. As an app creator, I take that same instinct for clarity and ship it directly to users — most recently with ThinkBefore, a web app that helps people think before they spend.

My academic path is unconventional and that's exactly what shapes how I work: a Level 3 certification in Data Analysis, a Bachelor's degree in Accounting, and a License in Arabic and Hebrew Literature. Numbers, structure and language — three different disciplines that all come down to the same skill: making complex things easy to understand.

In my free time, I read, explore new cultures, and keep experimenting with tech projects that push me to learn something new.
