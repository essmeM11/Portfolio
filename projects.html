📱 ThinkBefore — Spend with intention

A web app that fights impulse buying by converting any price into the number of work hours it actually costs you. Enter a price and your monthly income, and ThinkBefore shows the real cost in time — turning a quick purchase into a conscious decision. Launched on Product Hunt and the Chrome Web Store.

📊 Power BI — Apple's revenue

Collected Apple's revenue dataset from bullfincher.io, cleaned and transformed it for consistency across regions and fiscal years, then designed an interactive dashboard visualising revenue distribution across the Americas, Europe, Greater China, Japan and Asia-Pacific for 2022–2024. Used bar charts for regional comparison, a donut chart for yearly snapshots, stacked columns and line charts for trend analysis, and a filled map with pie markers to connect data to geography. Findings: the Americas remain the largest market but show slight decline, Europe shows steady growth, and Greater China shows a consistent decrease worth investigating — leading to recommendations on marketing investment and growth forecasting.
