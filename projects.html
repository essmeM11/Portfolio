App Development Live

📱 ThinkBefore — Spend with intention

A web app that fights impulse buying by converting any price into the number of work hours it actually costs you. Enter a price and your monthly income, and ThinkBefore shows the real cost in time — turning a quick purchase into a conscious decision. Built and shipped end-to-end: pricing tiers, a 48h wishlist to let urges fade, monthly spending charts, a goal tracker, CSV export, and a companion Chrome extension that surfaces the calculator while shopping online. Launched on Product Hunt and the Chrome Web Store.

📊 Power BI — Apple's Revenue Dashboard

Collected Apple's revenue dataset from bullfincher.io, cleaned and transformed it for consistency across regions and fiscal years, then designed an interactive dashboard visualising revenue distribution across the Americas, Europe, Greater China, Japan and Asia-Pacific for 2022–2024. Key findings: the Americas remain the largest market but show slight decline; Europe shows steady growth; Greater China shows a consistent decrease — leading to recommendations on marketing investment reallocation and regional growth forecasting.
